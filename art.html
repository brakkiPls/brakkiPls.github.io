Seasonal Drawings


Here you can see the Seasonal Drawings!
Each month there is a different selection of 3 template drawings that you can get me to draw, but you can customize these a lot more than a classic YCH.
The Price for any Seasonal Drawing is $30 or 6 gift subs.

You can:
- Chose your own Character!
- Add accessoires if wanted! (Bandana, Hat, ...)
- Chose your individual Pose and Expression!
- Chose painted or illustrated style (Sample below)
- Add an extra character! (= 50$ or 10 gift subs)
- Ask me any more questions you have.

You can not:
- Change the main idea of the drawing
- Rush me. I can take my time.
- Force me to accept your commission.
- Get a refund. Make sure you actually want this!

Make sure to check out the samples to see if you are interested in getting a Seasonal Drawing of your character!
If you are, please message me on Discord! (@brakkie)

Please make sure you notify me before you give me money to make sure that your idea is ok!
