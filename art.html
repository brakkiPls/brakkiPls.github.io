Seasonal Drawings


Here you can see the Seasonal Drawings!
Each month there is a different selection of 3 YCH like drawings that you can get me to draw, but you can customize these a lot more than the classic YCH.

You can:

- chose your own Character!
- add accessoires if wanted! (Bandana, Hat, ...)
- chose your individual Pose and Expression!
- chose painted or illustrated style (Example below)
- etc ...

You can not:

- add a second character! (Normal comission prices apply for this)
- change the main idea of the drawing

Also: These Samples are just Sketches! The actual Art you Comission will be a lot higher quality.

If you are interested in getting a Seasonal Drawing of your Character, please message me on Discord (@brakkie).

The Price for any Seasonal Drawing is $25.
